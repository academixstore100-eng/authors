Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to have published before?

No. Most of our authors are first-time writers. If you know your subject and can explain it clearly, our editorial team helps with the rest.

How much can I earn?

Royalties depend on your book's price and sales volume. We'll walk you through the royalty model in detail once your application is reviewed.

Do I retain the rights to my work?

Yes, authorship and credit remain yours. Full licensing terms are shared and agreed upon before you begin writing.

How long does it take to publish a book?

It varies by subject and length, but most first books go from outline to published in 8–12 weeks.

Can I co-author with a colleague?

Yes. Mention this in your application under "why do you want to become an author" and we'll set up both of you.
